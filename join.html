Everyone is Welcome!

JSA is open to everyone and anyone who is interested in Japan and Japanese culture. You DO NOT need to be Japanese or speak Japanese to join JSA. We welcome anyone who is interested to reach out to us, let us know, and check out our events!

Get in Touch

We'd like to get to know you! If you would like to join the listserv, ask us a question, or just say hi, find us on Facebook! We look forward to hearing from you!
