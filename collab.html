Campus-wide Involvement

As an active member of the Asian Pacific Student Coalition and the United Minorities Council, we participate in various multi-cultural events. During APAHW (Asian Pacific American Heritage Week), JSA held a booth that introduced origami and calligraphy.

Always Open to New Projects

We are always looking to broaden our collaborative efforts and work with different organizations on a plethora of projects and ideas. If you have an idea for a collaborative event, please contact us at jsa.upenn [at] gmail.com or find us on Facebook !
